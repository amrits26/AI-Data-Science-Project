# Imperial Cars FAQ

**What warranties do you offer?**
We offer a 3-month/3,000-mile limited warranty on all retail vehicles, with extended warranty options available.

**Do you take trade-ins?**
Yes, we accept trade-ins and offer competitive market value. Bring your vehicle for an on-site appraisal.

**What is your doc fee?**
Our documentation fee is $250, which covers title, registration, and paperwork processing.

**Can I test drive a car?**
Absolutely! Bring your driver’s license and proof of insurance, and we’ll set up a test drive for any vehicle in stock.

**Do you offer financing?**
Yes, we work with a variety of lenders to help you secure the best rates and terms.

**Can I reserve a vehicle?**
Yes, a refundable deposit will hold a vehicle for up to 48 hours.

**Are your vehicles inspected?**
All vehicles undergo a 125-point inspection before being offered for sale.

**Do you offer service and repairs?**
Yes, our service department is open Monday–Saturday for maintenance and repairs.

**How do I get a Carfax report?**
Just ask! We provide free Carfax reports for all vehicles in our inventory.

**What forms of payment do you accept?**
We accept cash, cashier’s checks, and all major credit cards for down payments.
