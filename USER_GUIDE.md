# Imperial Cars AI - Team User Guide

## 1) Open the dashboard on tablet
1. Connect tablet to dealership Wi-Fi.
2. Open Chrome.
3. Enter URL given by your manager (example: http://10.20.0.91:8000).

## 2) Use the chatbot
Type plain questions like:
- "What is the towing capacity of a Silverado 1500?"
- "Compare Honda CR-V vs Toyota RAV4 for family use."
- "Estimate monthly payment for $28,000 with $4,000 down over 60 months."
- "What should I know before buying used?"

Tips:
- Ask one clear question at a time.
- If answer is too broad, ask follow-up: "Give me 3 bullet points".

## 3) Upload lead sheet photo (OCR)
In Salesperson Mode:
1. Open Paperwork tab.
2. Upload clear photo of the lead sheet.
3. Wait for extracted fields.
4. Verify names/phone/email before saving.

If Telegram flow is enabled in your store, follow your manager's Telegram upload process.

## 4) Payment estimator and trade-in tool
Payment Estimator:
1. Set price, down payment, APR, and term.
2. Show customer monthly estimate and total financed amount.

Trade-In Wizard:
1. Enter year, make, model.
2. Add mileage and condition.
3. Save lead and send resume link if customer leaves.

## 5) Paperwork workflow
1. Upload finance/insurance/commission documents.
2. Review OCR output.
3. Correct obvious OCR mistakes.
4. Continue with follow-up actions.

## 6) Daily briefing and lead scores
Use Activity Dashboard / Lifecycle tabs to view:
- Today activity counts
- Lead quality score/tier
- Contact cadence progress
- Daily goals status

## 7) Salesperson mode access
- Tap Salesperson Mode.
- Enter your PIN.
- If PIN fails, ask admin to verify salesperson PIN configuration.
